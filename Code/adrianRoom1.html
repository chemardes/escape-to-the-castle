Escape to the Castle

You have stumbled upon yet another room. The door suddenly closes SHUT! This room looks messy with writings on the walls. As you examine closer, the writings are not just any writings - they're math equations. This room is thought to be a room of a math teacher who lost their mind and the only way to open the door is to enter the code for the lock. There is a clue on the lock saying, "To leave the room you must solve the equation hidden in this room, written in invisible ink. You need to look for a dot like switch to reveal the equation".

Find ♠ to get out: (HINT: All number is less than 10)
♣ * ♣ - ♥ = 32
(♥ + ♥) * ♦ = 24
♠ + ♣ - ♥ - ♥ + ♦ = 6
